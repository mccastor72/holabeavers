He/She Pattern
-ar→ -a, -er→ -e, -ir→ -e. Example: habla, come, vive. This is the starting point for all conjugation.

Yo Form Rule
All regular verbs use -o in the yo form: hablo, como, vivo. No exceptions for regular verbs — only irregular verbs break this pattern.

Group Endings
