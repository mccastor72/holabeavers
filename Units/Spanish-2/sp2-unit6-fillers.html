Conversation Fillers

Keeping a conversation going

C
I must know...
Required for a C
🎯 Goals
  • Use basic filler words
  • Say No entiendo and ask for repetition
📖 Vocabulary
EspañolEnglishPronunciation
bueno...well...BWEH-noh
pues...well... / so...pwehs
No entiendo.I don't understand.noh ehn-TYEHN-doh
¿Puede repetir?Can you repeat?PWEH-deh reh-peh-TEER
Más despacio, por favor.Slower, please.mahs dehs-PAH-syoh
Creo que sí.I think so.KREH-oh keh see
Creo que no.I don't think so.KREH-oh keh noh
Tal vez.Maybe.tahl behs
📝 Grammar
Why Fillers Matter
Fillers like bueno and pues are the Spanish equivalent of 'um' and 'well.' Using them makes you sound more natural and gives you time to think.

Agreeing & Disagreeing
Estoy de acuerdo (I agree) / No estoy de acuerdo (I disagree). Add contigo (with you) for clarity: Estoy de acuerdo contigo.
