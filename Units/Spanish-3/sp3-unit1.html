El subjuntivo avanzado

Advanced Subjunctive

📅 Weeks 1–3

Learning Objectives

  • Use the subjunctive in adverbial clauses (para que, a menos que...)
  • Form and use the imperfect subjunctive
  • Distinguish present vs. imperfect subjunctive contexts
  • Express hypothetical situations with si clauses
  • Write complex sentences using subjunctive triggers

Grammar Explanations

Imperfect Subjunctive Formation
Take the ellos preterite form, drop -ron, add: -ra, -ras, -ra, -ramos, -rais, -ran. Example: hablar → hablaron → hablara, hablaras... Irregular preterites carry over: tener → tuvieron → tuviera.
Adverbial Conjunctions + Subjunctive
Certain conjunctions always require subjunctive: para que, a menos que, antes de que, sin que, con tal de que, en caso de que. Others use subjunctive only for future/hypothetical: cuando, aunque, donde.
Sequence of Tenses
Present subjunctive follows present/future main verbs: Espero que vengas. Imperfect subjunctive follows past/conditional main verbs: Esperaba que vinieras.
Si Clauses: Hypotheticals
For unlikely/hypothetical conditions: Si + imperfect subjunctive, + conditional. Example: Si tuviera dinero, viajaría al mundo. (If I had money, I would travel the world.)
