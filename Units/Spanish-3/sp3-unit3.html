Grammar Explanations

Essay Discourse Markers
Use these to structure written arguments: En primer lugar (first), Además (furthermore), Por otro lado (on the other hand), Sin embargo (however), En conclusión (in conclusion).
Passive Voice with Se
Use se + third person verb to express passive or impersonal meaning: Se habla español en 20 países. (Spanish is spoken in 20 countries.) Se valoran las tradiciones. (Traditions are valued.)
Comparisons of Equality/Inequality
tan + adj. + como (as ... as): Es tan importante como.... más/menos + adj. + que (more/less ... than): La diversidad es más valiosa que la uniformidad.
Nominalization
Turn adjectives into nouns using lo + adjective: lo importante (what is important / the important thing), lo difícil (the difficult part). Useful in academic writing.
