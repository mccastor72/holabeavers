Quisiera vs. Quiero
Use Quisiera ___ for polite ordering — it's the conditional of querer and much softer than quiero (I want). Always preferred in a restaurant.

📝 Grammar
Estar for Taste
Use estar to describe how food tastes: Está delicioso, está muy rico, está frío, está picante. Never use ser for taste descriptions.
Asking the Server
¿Qué recomienda? (What do you recommend?) is a great phrase that shows confidence in Spanish.
