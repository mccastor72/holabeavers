What to do when you don't know a word

C
I must know...
Required for a C
🎯 Goals
  • Ask how to say something in Spanish
  • Ask what a word means

EspañolEnglishPronunciation

¿Qué significa...?What does ... mean?keh seeg-NEE-fee-kah
¿Cómo se escribe?How do you spell it?KOH-moh seh ehs-KREE-beh

Se dice...You say... / It's said...seh DEE-seh
Significa...It means...seeg-NEE-fee-kah
📝 Grammar
Ask, Don't Stop
When you don't know a word, ask! ¿Cómo se dice 'butterfly' en español? Keeping the conversation going is more important than knowing every word.

Required for a B
🎯 Goals
  • Use circumlocution to describe words you don't know
📖 Vocabulary
EspañolEnglishPronunciation
Es una cosa que se usa para...It's a thing used to...ehs OO-nah KOH-sah keh seh OO-sah PAH-rah
Es como...It's like...ehs KOH-moh
Se parece a...It looks/seems like...seh pah-REH-seh ah
Es lo opuesto de...It's the opposite of...ehs loh oh-PWES-toh deh
Se me olvidó la palabra.I forgot the word.seh meh ohl-bee-DOH lah pah-LAH-brah
Me refiero a...I mean / I'm referring to...meh reh-FYEH-roh ah
📝 Grammar
Circumlocution
When you forget a word, describe it instead! Es una cosa que se usa para abrir puertas (It's a thing used to open doors = a key). This is a key skill for language proficiency.
