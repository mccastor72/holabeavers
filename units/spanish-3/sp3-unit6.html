Learning Objectives

Grammar Explanations

Tense Review: When to Use Each
Present: current facts/habits. Preterite: completed past events. Imperfect: background/ongoing past. Future: predictions/plans. Conditional: hypotheticals. Subjunctive: after triggers (wishes, doubt, emotion, impersonal expressions).
Conditional Perfect
Form: haber conditional + past participle: habría hablado (I would have spoken). Used for hypotheticals about the past: Si hubiera estudiado, habría sacado una A.
Nominalization and Abstract Language
Elevate your language with nominalization: instead of Es importante proteger el ambiente, say La protección del ambiente es crucial. Turn verbs into nouns for more academic register.
Cohesion Devices
Link ideas smoothly: Asimismo (likewise), De hecho (in fact), No obstante (nevertheless), A modo de conclusión (by way of conclusion), En síntesis (in summary).
