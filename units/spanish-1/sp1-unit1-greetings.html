📝 Grammar
Time-Based Greetings
Match the greeting to the time of day: buenos días (morning), buenas tardes (afternoon), buenas noches (evening/night). Hola works any time.
Basic Politeness
gracias (thank you), de nada (you're welcome), por favor (please) — essential for any interaction.

📝 Grammar
Casual Greetings
¿Qué tal? and ¿Qué pasa? are informal ways to say 'how's it going?' Common among friends.
Farewell Variety
adiós = final goodbye. hasta luego / nos vemos = see you later. hasta pronto = see you soon. Choose based on how soon you'll see them again.

📝 Grammar
Responding to Greetings
Don't just say bien — try: Bien, gracias. ¿Y tú? or Más o menos. ¿Y usted? Always ask back!
Regional Expressions
¿Qué onda? is common in Mexico. Ciao is used in many Spanish-speaking countries. These show cultural awareness.
